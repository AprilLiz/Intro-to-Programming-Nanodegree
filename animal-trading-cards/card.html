Siberian tiger

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigrisa ltaica
  • Average Size: 4 metres/300 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 15 years
  • Main Habitat: Russian Far East
  • Population Size: around 600

The Siberian tiger is reddish-rusty, or rusty-yellow in color, with narrow black transverse stripes. To keep themselves from becoming cold in the winters, Siberian tigers have thicker fur coats than tigers which live in southern Asia. They are also called "Amur tiger", "Manchurian tiger", "Korean tiger", and "Ussurian tiger", depending on the region where individuals were observed.
